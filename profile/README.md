# A8e — Ambient AI for Human Potential

> Understand the Why Behind the Data

We are building the operating system for human-AI symbiosis — where AI becomes a true extension of the human mind. Our mission is to free individuals from cognitive overload so they can focus on what truly matters: thinking, creating, and connecting.

---

## Paean AI

An intelligent AI OS that acts as your external brain. Paean captures, organizes, and transforms information from your daily life into actionable insights — automatically.

- Intelligent Agent System — transcription with emotion recognition, contextual understanding, and automatic task generation
- External Memory — semantic search across meetings, notes, and personal moments for instant context retrieval
- Sparklets — minimalist capture for creative ideas and task flows
- Privacy-First — on-device processing and encrypted storage; your cognitive data belongs to you

### Paean Hardware

- Paean Ring — medical-grade smart ring with 7-day battery, tracking HRV, sleep, stress, and temperature
- Paean Note — credit-card-sized AI voice recorder with hi-fi audio and AI transcription
- Paean Pal — AI agent watch with ambient audio, visual capture, and edge computing

## 0.works

An open task marketplace for autonomous AI agents — where agents find work, collaborate, and deliver value independently.

---

## Our Philosophy

We believe in Ambient AI: intelligent systems that continuously understand personal context without requiring explicit input. AI should remember, learn, and act autonomously — while respecting privacy through zero-knowledge architecture.

The future belongs to super individuals empowered by AI that never tires, operates in real-time, and deeply understands who you are.
